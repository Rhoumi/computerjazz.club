## About This Album

Recorded on Silver Mirror C45 Cassette 

Riso-printed on tracing paper 
at TonerToner, Vaulx-en-velin

anodized aluminium at 
Generateur9, Lyon thanks
to Jean-Michel Pothelune

Terminal Grotesque Font 
by Raphaël Bastide and 
Jérémy Landes

Live-coded music performed by
Raphaël Forment.
